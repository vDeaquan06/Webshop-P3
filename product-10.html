Miles Morales debuted in 2011 within Marvels Ultimate Universe as a successor to Peter Parker. Created by Brian Michael Bendis and Sara Pichelli, Miles is a biracial teenager (Afro-Latino) from Brooklyn who gains spider powers and eventually takes up the Spider-Man mantle. After the Ultimate Universe collapsed, Miles transitioned into Marvels main continuity, headlining series such as Miles Morales: Spider-Man. His stories focus on coming-of-age struggles, family relationships, cultural identity, and living up to the legacy of Spider-Man while defining his own version of heroism. Miles possesses similar powers to Peter Parker but also unique abilities like bio-electric “venom” blasts and camouflage. The series is celebrated for modern storytelling, diverse representation, and its exploration of legacy, mentorship, and self-discovery.
