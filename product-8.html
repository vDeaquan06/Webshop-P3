Steel Ball Run (20042011) is Part 7 of Hirohiko Arakis long-running JoJos Bizarre Adventure saga and marks a major reboot of the franchises continuity. Set in an alternate-universe 1890s America, the story follows paraplegic former jockey Johnny Joestar and mysterious racer Gyro Zeppeli as they compete in a cross-continental horse race known as the Steel Ball Run. The series blends western, adventure, and supernatural elements through Arakis signature Stand-based combat system. Unlike earlier parts, Steel Ball Run emphasizes character-driven storytelling, moral complexity, and a more grounded emotional tone.
