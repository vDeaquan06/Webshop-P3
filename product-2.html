Written and illustrated by Makoto Yukimura, Vinland Saga began serialization in 2005 and remains ongoing. Set during the Viking Age in the early 11th century, the manga initially follows Thorfinn, a young Icelandic warrior seeking revenge against mercenary leader Askeladd for killing his father. What begins as a brutal revenge narrative gradually transforms into a philosophical exploration of violence, pacifism, and the search for meaning. Major story arcs include the War Arc, Slave Arc, Eastern Expedition Arc, and Vinland Arc. Each phase shifts tone—from battlefield brutality to introspective character growth—reflecting Thorfinns evolution from vengeance-driven child to a man pursuing peace. Renowned for historical accuracy, detailed artwork, and emotionally grounded storytelling, Vinland Saga explores themes such as the cost of war, leadership, redemption, and the pursuit of a utopian land free from conflict: Vinland.
