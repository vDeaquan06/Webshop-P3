The story follows Eren Yeager, Mikasa Ackerman, and Armin Arlert as they join the military to fight Titans after witnessing catastrophic destruction. Early arcs focus on survival horror and mystery, while later arcs expand into political intrigue, war, and philosophical debates about freedom and morality.
