Serialized from 2018 to 2024 and created by Gege Akutami, Jujutsu Kaisen is a modern dark-fantasy battle manga that quickly became one of the defining shōnen series of its generation. The story centers on Yuji Itadori, a physically gifted teenager who becomes host to the powerful curse Ryomen Sukuna and is pulled into the hidden world of jujutsu sorcerers—individuals who combat supernatural curses born from human negativity. The series blends high-speed action with horror aesthetics, moral ambiguity, and a sophisticated power system based on cursed energy and techniques. Major arcs include the Cursed Womb, Kyoto Goodwill Event, Shibuya Incident, and Culling Game arcs, each escalating stakes and character development.
