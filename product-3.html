Takehiko Inoues Vagabond began in 1998 and is currently on indefinite hiatus. Inspired by the novel Musashi, it fictionalizes the life of legendary Japanese swordsman Miyamoto Musashi. The manga follows Musashis journey from a violent, reckless youth to a disciplined warrior seeking enlightenment through swordsmanship. Rather than focusing solely on combat, the story emphasizes personal growth, philosophy, and the search for meaning. Famous for its breathtaking, highly detailed artwork and cinematic paneling, Vagabond examines themes such as strength, ego, solitude, and self-mastery. It is widely considered one of the greatest seinen manga ever created due to its mature storytelling and artistic excellence.
